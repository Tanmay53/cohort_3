We have a well-defined admission process so that we only get the highly motivated and deserving students in the course. It is defined step by step as follows:
Phase I Apply on the website: https://admissions.masaischool.com and complete the application process which consists of two parts. Every part has a well-thought objective to be assessed.
Phase II Once you complete the above step, we will evaluate your application and if you scored above the cut off score, you sign an admission letter for joining the Pre-course (1 week duration). Pre-course is a mandatory course for all the
students. Phase III If you qualify the above Phase II, then you will get a call from the admissions team at Masai School for a 15 minutes face-to-face or Skype interview.

In our entire admission process, we are only looking for one thing: motivation to learn and earn. Our course is very intensive and demanding, and we want the most motivated people to get onboard. Make sure that you explain why you want to join Masai School in your application - the more details you give us, the better chance you will have to get selected for the interview!
