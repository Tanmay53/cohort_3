TCP/IP  Transmission Control Protocol/Internetprotocol:
IT having Fout layers:

1.Network Access Layer:
    it encoded the  data  into bits and then converted into electrical impulses.
2.Internet Layer:
    it defines the protocols which are responsible for logical transmission of data over the network.
3.Host-TO-Host Layer:
    it is responsible for data transfer one to another,error free delevery of data.
4.Process Layer:
    it is responsible for node to node communication and control the user-interface.
